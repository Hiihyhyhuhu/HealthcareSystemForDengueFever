# Kid Healthcare System

## Introduction
*Kid Healthcare System* is a web-based healthcare platform designed to help parents monitor and manage their children's health, with a focus on the early detection of *HFMD (Hand, Foot, and Mouth Disease)*

The system aims to assist parents in recognizing early symptoms, reduce hospital registration time, and provide instant support through an intelligent chatbot. Therefore, reducing the overall rate of having HFMD in children

---

## Features
### 1. Pre-Diagnosis Using Computer Vision

- Detect visible *HFMD-related symptoms* from *images or camera from computers and smartphones*
- Detecting potential *HFMD symptoms* such as skin rashes or mouth ulcers and providing *early risk indication* for parents
- Support doctors with *preliminary screening data*
- Encourage *early medical consultation*

---

### 2. Online Appointment Booking
The platform provides an *online patient information form* for booking medical appointments

Parents can:
- Enter the child's information
- Describe symptoms
- Select an appointment time

Benefits:
- Doctors can *review patient information in advance*
- Patients can *skip traditional hospital registration procedures*
- Reduced waiting time at healthcare facilities

---

### 3. Healthcare Chatbot
The system integrates a *medical chatbot* to assist users

The chatbot can:
- Answer common questions about *HFMD*
- Provide *basic care recommendations*
- Suggest *when to visit a doctor*
- Guide users in using the system
